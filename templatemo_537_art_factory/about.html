About Snails

Snails are a very common type of gastropod mollusk. While most live on land, some snails can live in fresh or salt water. The defining characteristic of the land snail is its shell. These shells serve as a cozy home where snails can hide from predators and come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and designs. There are thousands of different varieties of land snails that live in moist areas all over the world.
